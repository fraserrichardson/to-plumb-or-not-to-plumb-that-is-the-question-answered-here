The thread

The same job, in four settings.

Make a proposition feel distinctive to a specific audience. Hold a consistent look and tone across every surface that carries the logo. Brief creative teams to work to that standard without bottlenecking on me.

That is what your JD is asking for, and it is what I have actually been doing.

Why Plum, specifically

Savings and investing is a category where verbal identity genuinely drives trust.

You are running at European scale across ten markets, which is the kind of multilingual positioning problem I find interesting after four years working out of Monaco.

"Grow money for life" is a clearer brand proposition than most fintechs manage. And a refresh that has to reflect a growing product suite and an international presence is exactly the kind of work I want to be closest to.

On AI

A working tool, not a novelty.

Claude and ChatGPT are part of my daily workflow for drafting, research, landing-page iteration, brand-voice calibration, and campaign analysis.

I use them to hold consistent tone across a lot of output, which is close to the operational problem a Brand Lead overseeing creative at scale is trying to solve.
